Public Health Assessment Framework

Comprehensive Health Systems Analysis for South Asian Contexts
Practical Tool for Health Practitioners, Policy Makers & Development Organizations

📋 How to Use This Assessment Framework

  1. Scope Definition: Identify geographic area, population, and health priorities
  2. Data Collection: Gather quantitative data and conduct stakeholder interviews
  3. Building Block Analysis: Rate each health system component (1-5 scale)
  4. Equity Assessment: Analyze disparities across population groups
  5. Gap Identification: Identify priority areas for intervention
  6. Action Planning: Develop targeted improvement strategies

Time Required: 2-4 weeks for comprehensive assessment | Team: 3-5 people with diverse expertise

7. Data Sources & Tools

📊 Key Data Sources for South Asian Health Systems

National Surveys:

  • National Family Health Survey (NFHS)
  • Annual Health Survey (AHS)
  • District Level Household Survey (DLHS)
  • Coverage Evaluation Survey (CES)

Routine Information Systems:

  • Health Management Information System (HMIS)
  • Integrated Disease Surveillance Programme (IDSP)
  • Mother and Child Tracking System (MCTS)
  • Electronic Vaccine Intelligence Network (eVIN)

Administrative Data:

  • Rural Health Statistics (Ministry of Health)
  • National Health Accounts
  • Public Financial Management System
  • Human Resources for Health data

International Sources:

  • WHO Global Health Observatory
  • World Bank Health Data
  • Institute for Health Metrics (IHME)
  • UN Inter-agency Group estimates

🛠️ Assessment Tools & Methodologies

  • WHO Health Systems Assessment Tool: Comprehensive framework for system analysis
  • Service Availability and Readiness Assessment (SARA): Facility-level capacity
  • Health Equity Assessment Toolkit: WHO guidance on equity analysis
  • OneHealth Assessment for Planning and Performance (OH-APP): Multi-sectoral tool
  • Community Health Assessment Toolkit: Participatory approaches
  • Health System Resilience Assessment: Emergency preparedness evaluation
