# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

ImpactMojo is an educational platform serving development practitioners, researchers, students, and educators across South Asia and beyond. We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, ethnicity, religion, age, or experience level.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Welcome newcomers and help them get started
- Give and receive feedback graciously
- Focus on what is best for the community and for learning
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Trolling, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Spam, self-promotion unrelated to development education, or commercial solicitation
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all ImpactMojo spaces: GitHub repository, Discord server, Telegram channel, WhatsApp PLC, and any other community spaces.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **hello@impactmojo.in**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. The project team reserves the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct, and to temporarily or permanently ban any contributor.
