Climate Action Challenge

Manage your region's climate strategy across 10 decades. Balance mitigation and adaptation to protect your people.

The Challenge

Climate change demands two kinds of action: Mitigation (reducing emissions to slow warming) and Adaptation (building resilience to survive impacts). Each round, you receive 100 climate credits to allocate. Neither strategy alone is enough — you must find the right balance.

Mitigation Actions

Solar energy, reforestation, carbon capture, clean transport — slow down warming for long-term benefit.

Adaptation Actions

Flood barriers, drought-resistant crops, early warning systems, cooling infrastructure — protect lives now.
