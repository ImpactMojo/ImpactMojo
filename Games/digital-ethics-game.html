The Algorithm's Dilemma

Navigate 8 real-world AI ethics scenarios

How It Works

  • You will face 8 scenarios involving AI and algorithmic systems
  • Each scenario presents 3–4 approaches to a real ethical dilemma
  • Your choices affect 4 dimensions: Efficiency, Fairness, Privacy, Public Trust
  • There is no perfect solution — every choice involves tradeoffs
  • Your cumulative dashboard reveals your design philosophy

The Four Dimensions

Efficiency
How well does the system achieve its intended purpose?
Fairness
Does the system treat all groups equitably?
Privacy
How well are individual data rights protected?
Public Trust
Does the public believe the system is legitimate?
