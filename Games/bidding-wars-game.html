Bidding Wars

Auction Theory & The Winner's Curse

How It Works

In a sealed-bid first-price auction, every bidder submits one secret bid. The highest bidder wins and pays exactly what they bid.

  • Each round, an item with a hidden true value goes up for auction.
  • You receive a noisy estimate of the value (±20% of truth).
  • You and 3 AI agents bid simultaneously. Highest bid wins.
  • Winner's profit = True Value − Your Bid

The Winner's Curse

When everyone has imperfect information, the person who wins the auction is often the one who most overestimated the item's value. Winning can actually mean losing money. This is the Winner's Curse.
