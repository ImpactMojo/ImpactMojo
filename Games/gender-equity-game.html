The Care Economy Challenge

Experience the invisible burden of unpaid care work and discover how policy choices shape gender equity outcomes.

How to play: You manage a household over 8 years. Each year, allocate 24 time units across activities:
  • Paid work — earns income, builds career
  • Childcare — affects child wellbeing
  • Eldercare — affects family health
  • Household work — cooking, cleaning, maintenance
  • Education — boosts future earning power
  • Rest & self-care — prevents burnout

The twist: In the first 3 years, time is pre-allocated reflecting systemic inequality. From year 4 onward, you redesign the distribution. Policy events will shape what is possible.
