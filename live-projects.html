What are Live Projects?

Live Projects are interactive, data-driven dashboards that pull from real-world APIs and open datasets. Each project visualises development and climate data relevant to South Asia — making complex datasets accessible and explorable. All projects are open-source, require no login, and are designed as learning companions to ImpactMojo courses.
