So why are we writing this letter?

Because we've seen a pattern. The people who learn the most on ImpactMojo are the ones who get stuck at the same point.

They finish a course on Development Economics. They understand DiD, RDD, IV estimation. They can explain the theory. But when they sit down to actually run a difference-in-differences analysis on their project data — they're stuck. They need practice datasets. They need code that works. They need someone to check their logic.

They complete MEL. They understand Theory of Change, LogFrames, indicators. But when their programme director asks them to build an actual ToC for a new grant proposal due next week — they need the professional workbench, not the introductory lab.

They learn about qualitative research methods. They understand thematic coding. But when they're sitting with 47 FGD transcripts in Hindi and Bengali and need to produce a thematic analysis for a donor report — they need VaniScribe and the Qual Insights Lab.

This is the gap that Premium fills. Not more theory. Not more lectures. Professional tools that turn your learning into real-world output.

Let us be specific about what that looks like.
