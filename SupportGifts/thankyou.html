Thank You for Your Support

Your contribution strengthens development education across South Asia

We are deeply grateful for your support of ImpactMojo and our mission to democratize access to rigorous, contextualized development knowledge. Your contribution directly enables us to maintain this platform as a free, open-access resource for educators, practitioners, and researchers across South Asia.

Every donation helps us expand our library of knowledge decks, develop new teaching materials, and ensure that critical concepts in development economics, social justice, and public policy remain accessible to those who need them most—without paywalls, without barriers.

As a token of our appreciation, we're providing you with immediate access to our complete collection of supplementary handouts and teaching materials. These resources are designed to complement our core knowledge decks and can be used in classrooms, workshops, and self-directed learning.
