What these are

Each timeline is a hand-curated, chronologically-ordered map of a domain — not a wiki dump. Every node carries: the original argument, why it mattered, the critique that came after, and links to where you can study the idea more deeply on ImpactMojo (101 decks, BookSummary companions, Deep Dives, Handouts).

What they aren’t

Not a substitute for primary sources. Each node cites the canonical text and its key critics. Use these as orientation maps before diving into a 101 deck or BookSummary — or as a way to see how the ideas in a single book/paper sit in a longer intellectual lineage.
