Practitioner Starter Kit

10 essential handouts to keep on your desk. Research design, M&E frameworks, data analysis, policy tracking — the ones practitioners actually use.

01Research Design Worksheet

M&E

M&E

Systematically test every assumption in your Theory of Change. The checklist that stops you presenting a ToC full of wishful thinking to your donor.

03Case Study Template

Cross-cutting

A structured template for writing programme case studies. Background, methodology, findings, lessons learned — ready for your annual report or donor presentation.

04Data Literacy Essentials

Data

Data

07Bivariate Analysis Guide

Data

When to use chi-square vs. t-test vs. correlation. A practical decision tree for choosing the right test for your data.

08Policy Tracking Sheet

Policy

Track policy developments across sectors and geographies. Useful for advocacy teams monitoring legislative changes that affect their programmes.

09Development Communications Guide

Cross-cutting

How to write for donors, communities, and media. Covers everything from executive summaries to field stories to social media posts.

10Software Tools Guide for Development

The best free and affordable tools for survey design, data analysis, project management, and visualization. What to use when, and why.
