Why We Built ImpactMojo

After years of field research and teaching across South Asia, we kept encountering the same frustration: rigorous development education was either locked behind expensive degrees or scattered across dense academic papers. Practitioners in the field—the people actually implementing programs—deserved better.

We built ImpactMojo to bridge that gap—PhD-level content with practitioner-level accessibility. Every course, lab, and game is designed for people who need to apply these concepts tomorrow, not just understand them in theory. It's playful, earthy, wise, and accessible—because learning shouldn't feel like suffering.

ImpactMojo is an open-source educational platform dedicated to democratizing access to development knowledge and social impact education across South Asia and beyond.
