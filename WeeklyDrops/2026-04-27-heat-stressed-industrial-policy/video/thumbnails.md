# 5 Thumbnail concepts — The Heat Floor

All concepts: 1280×720, ImpactMojo orange/teal palette, large legible headline overlay (≤ 5 words), face or hand element when possible (boosts CTR materially).

---

## #1 — **The Split** (LEAD)

**Composition:** Vertical split. Left half: a clean, glossy factory — air-conditioned, well-lit, "winning" energy. Right half: an informal worker outside in heat haze, sweat beading, pouring water on her head. A bright red thermometer slices vertically down the middle, mercury rising into red zone.

**Headline overlay (top-right):** **THE HEAT FLOOR**
**Sub-overlay (bottom-left):** *"259 billion lost hours"*

**Napkin prompt:**
> Split-frame editorial illustration, 16:9. Left half: cool blue-toned factory floor with workers at calm assembly line, air-conditioning units visible. Right half: warm orange-red toned informal construction site, woman labourer outdoors pouring water on her head, heat shimmer, sun blazing. Red mercury thermometer cutting vertically through the centre, mercury rising into red. Hand-drawn editorial style, ImpactMojo orange and teal accents, paper texture background.

**Why this is the lead:** Single visual idea, immediate tension, both halves legible at thumbnail size, high CTR pattern (split + face).

---

## #2 — **The Number Drop**

**Composition:** Black background. Massive white type: **259 BILLION** centred. Underneath in smaller orange type: *hours/year India loses to heat*. A small thermometer icon top-right. ImpactMojo logo bottom.

**Headline overlay:** Already integral to the design.
**Sub-overlay:** *"What industrial policy skips"*

**Napkin prompt:**
> Minimalist editorial thumbnail, 16:9, deep charcoal background. Massive bold sans-serif numerals "259 BILLION" centred in white. Smaller orange italic caption beneath: "hours/year India loses to heat." Tiny red thermometer icon top-right. ImpactMojo wordmark bottom-right. Newspaper editorial aesthetic, high-contrast, paper texture overlay.

**Use case:** Strong on text-heavy feeds (LinkedIn embeds, newsletter previews).

---

## #3 — **The Wet-Bulb Wage Slip**

**Composition:** A close-up of a worker's wage slip, printed on lined paper, with a "HEAT-DAY DEDUCTION" line stamped in red ink. A faint heat-haze gradient through the page.

**Headline overlay (top):** **40% LESS**
**Sub-overlay (bottom-right):** *"on heatwave days"*

**Napkin prompt:**
> Editorial close-up of an Indian worker's payslip on lined yellow paper, fountain-pen entries, with a bold red rubber stamp reading "HEAT-DAY DEDUCTION −40%". Subtle heat-haze gradient warping the upper edge. Realistic paper texture, rupee symbol visible in the column header. ImpactMojo orange accent on the stamp. 16:9, hand-drawn editorial style.

**Use case:** Higher CTR among labour-rights audience; tactile object draws clicks.

---

## #4 — **The Manufacturing Map**

**Composition:** India outline in dark teal. Manufacturing-cluster dots in orange. A heat overlay (red gradient) covering ~70% of the country, overlapping with most clusters. A magnifying glass hovering over one cluster shows a tiny thermometer hitting red.

**Headline overlay:** **WHERE THE FACTORIES ARE**
**Sub-overlay:** *"…and where the heat is"*

**Napkin prompt:**
> Stylised editorial map of India, 16:9, dark teal landmass on cream background. Bright orange dots marking manufacturing clusters across northern, western, and southern India. A translucent red gradient heat overlay covering most of the central plains, overlapping with most clusters. A round magnifying glass icon hovering over one cluster, revealing a small thermometer in red zone. Hand-drawn editorial cartography style, paper texture.

**Use case:** Geographic curiosity hook; performs well in policy/research feeds.

---

## #5 — **The Three Levers**

**Composition:** Three large icons side-by-side: a fan/AC unit (cooling subsidy), an indoor desk (services jobs), an umbrella over a worker (heat insurance). A dotted line links them. Above: "**3 REDESIGNS**". Below: "*for industrial policy*".

**Headline overlay (top):** **3 REDESIGNS**
**Sub-overlay (bottom):** *"…that fit the heat floor"*

**Napkin prompt:**
> Editorial illustration, 16:9, three hand-drawn icons in a horizontal row on cream paper texture. Icon 1 (left): industrial cooling fan/AC unit with downward arrows. Icon 2 (centre): an indoor office desk with a person working. Icon 3 (right): an umbrella sheltering a labourer, rupee symbol falling from above. Connected by a dotted line. Bold sans-serif heading "3 REDESIGNS" above, italic subtitle "…that fit the heat floor" below. ImpactMojo orange and teal accents.

**Use case:** Lower CTR but higher retention/completion — viewers self-select for the policy-design audience.
