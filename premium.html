101-level courses across policy, research methods, data literacy, and field practice. Covers everything from climate science and gender studies to behavioral economics and impact storytelling.

Explore DevDiscourses →

Practitioner
₹399/month · ₹3,990/year (2 months free)

Includes everything in Explorer, plus:

Research Question Builder Pro

A guided research question builder that helps you move from a vague idea to a testable, fundable question. Walks you through PICO/SPIDER framing, ensures your question is scoped to available data, and suggests appropriate methods. Useful for proposal writing, thesis design, and evaluation planning.

Theory of Change Workbench Pro

Build, iterate, and export publication-ready theories of change. Includes assumption mapping, evidence linkage for each causal pathway, and export to PDF/PNG for donor reports and proposals. Goes well beyond the free TOC lab with version history and collaborative features.

Completion Certificates

Professional
₹999/month · ₹9,990/year (2 months free)

Includes everything in Practitioner, plus:

Qualitative Insights Lab Pro

AI-assisted qualitative analysis for interview and FGD transcripts. Upload transcripts and get thematic coding suggestions, memo generation, pattern identification, and cross-case comparisons. Supports iterative coding with human-in-the-loop validation. Built for development researchers who need rigorous qual analysis without NVivo licensing costs. Launch Qual Insights Lab →

Statistical Code Converter Pro

Translate statistical code between R, Stata, SPSS, and Python with automatic documentation. Includes regression diagnostics, effect size calculators, and power analysis covering OLS, logistic regression, DiD, RDD, and IV estimation. Launch Code Converter →

VaniScribe: AI Transcription for Development

Transcribe field interviews and focus group discussions in Hindi, Tamil, Bengali, Marathi, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Gujarati, Odia, Punjabi, and more South Asian languages. Speaker diarization separates voices automatically. Export clean transcripts ready for qualitative coding.

DevData Practice: Realistic Development Datasets

36 realistic dataset generators producing 840,000+ rows modeled on the structure of DHS, NFHS, ASER, MGNREGA, and other major development surveys. Practice cleaning, merging, and analyzing data without confidentiality concerns. Each generator includes a data dictionary and suggested analysis exercises.

Visualization Cookbook: Chart Recipes for Development Data

14 chart types with question-driven recipes. Start with what you want to show ("How has enrollment changed over time?") and get production-ready code in R (ggplot2), Python (matplotlib/seaborn), and Excel. Each recipe includes design rationale, accessibility guidance, and export options for reports and presentations.

DevEconomics Toolkit: Interactive Shiny Apps

11 interactive Shiny apps covering core development economics methods: RCT power calculator, DiD event study simulator, RDD bandwidth selector, synthetic control builder, Foster-Greer-Thorbecke (FGT) poverty measures, Theil/MPI inequality decomposition, cost-effectiveness analysis, benefit incidence analysis, and more. Each app includes worked examples from South Asian datasets.

Priority Coaching & Early Access

Direct coaching access to Varna and Vandana for research design questions, career guidance, and methodology support. Plus early access to every new tool, course, and feature before public release.
