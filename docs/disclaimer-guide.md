## What ImpactMojo Is

ImpactMojo is an **educational platform**. We provide courses, tools, games, labs, and resources to help development professionals learn and improve their practice.

## What ImpactMojo Is Not

### Not professional advice

Our content is educational — it teaches general principles and best practices. It is **not** a substitute for:

- **Legal advice.** If you need guidance on contracts, compliance, or regulations, consult a qualified lawyer.
- **Financial advice.** If you're making investment, budgeting, or financial planning decisions, consult a qualified financial professional.
- **Medical or health advice.** Our public health content is educational. For clinical decisions, consult healthcare professionals.
- **Employment advice.** For hiring, HR policy, or labour law questions, consult HR and legal professionals.

### Not a guarantee of outcomes

Completing ImpactMojo courses does not guarantee:
- Employment, internships, or promotions
- Acquisition of specific skills (learning depends on how you engage with the material)
- That our approaches will work in your specific context (development settings vary enormously)

### Not an accredited institution

ImpactMojo is not a university, college, or government-recognised credentialing body. Our certificates certify course completion only. See [Certificates and Progress](certificates-and-progress.md) for the full disclaimer on what our certificates do and don't represent.
