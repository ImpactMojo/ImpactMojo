# Refund Policy — In Plain Language

This is a plain-language summary of ImpactMojo's refund policy. The full document is at [impactmojo.in/refund-policy.html](https://www.impactmojo.in/refund-policy.html).

---

## The Short Version

Free content is free — no refund needed. For paid services, we have fair cancellation windows. We're not trying to keep your money if the service didn't work for you.

---

## Free Content

All courses, games, labs, handouts, and resources on the free tier are permanently free. There's nothing to refund.

**Voluntary contributions** ("Pay What You Think Is Fair") are treated as donations. They're generally non-refundable, but if you have a concern, email us and we'll work it out.

---

## Workshop Refunds

| When you cancel | What you get |
|----------------|-------------|
| **7+ days before** the workshop | 100% refund |
| **3–7 days before** | 50% refund |
| **Less than 3 days before** | No refund |
| **No-show without notice** | No refund |

**Alternatives to a refund:**
- Transfer your spot to the next workshop session (free)
- Send a substitute from your organisation
- Apply the payment as credit toward a future service

---

## Coaching Session Refunds

| When you cancel | What you get |
|----------------|-------------|
| **48+ hours before** | 100% refund or reschedule |
| **24–48 hours before** | 50% refund or reschedule |
| **Less than 24 hours before** | No refund (but you can reschedule once) |

**Coaching packages:** Credits are valid for 12 months. If you have unused sessions, you can get a prorated refund. Sessions already completed are non-refundable.

---

## Premium Membership Refunds

### Monthly plans
- Cancel anytime — your access continues until the end of the current billing period
- No partial-month refunds

### Annual plans
- **Within the first 30 days:** Prorated refund
- **After 30 days:** No refund, but you can cancel and your access continues until the plan expires

### Organisation plans
- Custom terms based on your agreement — contact us to discuss

---

## If We Cancel Something

If ImpactMojo cancels a workshop, coaching session, or service:
- You get a **100% refund** or the option to transfer to another session
- We'll notify you as soon as possible
- Refund processing takes 7–10 business days

---

## Exceptional Circumstances

Life happens. If you need to cancel due to:
- Medical emergencies
- Family crises
- Natural disasters
- Travel disruptions

Email us and we'll consider full refunds, transfers, or credits on a case-by-case basis. We're reasonable people.

---

## How to Request a Refund

1. **Email** [hello@impactmojo.in](mailto:hello@impactmojo.in) with "Refund Request" in the subject line
2. **Include:** Your order or registration details and the reason for the request
3. **We'll acknowledge** within 2 business days
4. **Processing** takes 7–10 business days after approval
5. **Bank processing** may add 3–5 additional days
