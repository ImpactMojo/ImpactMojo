# Press Kit

ImpactMojo's press kit provides journalists, partners, and collaborators with everything they need to write about or reference the platform.

## What's in the Press Kit

The press kit page ([ImpactMojo_PressKit.html](/ImpactMojo_PressKit.html)) includes:

- **Brand assets** — logos, colour palette, and typography guidelines
- **Key facts** — platform statistics, mission statement, and founding story
- **Media-ready descriptions** — short, medium, and long descriptions for press use
- **Contact information** — media enquiries and partnership requests

## How to Access

The press kit is available at [impactmojo.in/ImpactMojo_PressKit.html](https://www.impactmojo.in/ImpactMojo_PressKit.html) and is linked from the About Us navigation menu and site footer.

## For Partners

If you're writing about ImpactMojo or considering a partnership, the press kit is your starting point. For additional assets or custom requests, reach out via [hello@impactmojo.in](mailto:hello@impactmojo.in).
