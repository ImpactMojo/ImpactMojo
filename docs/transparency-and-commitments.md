# Transparency & Commitments

What ImpactMojo commits to — and how we hold ourselves accountable.

---

## Our Core Commitments

### 1. Free Content Stays Free

All 9 flagship courses, 39 foundational courses, 12 games, 10 labs, 400+ handouts, ImpactLex, Dev Case Studies, DevDiscourses, and the Dataverse are free. This is not a promotional offer or a trial period — it's the foundation of the platform.

**What this means in practice:**
- We will never put existing free content behind a paywall
- Premium tools exist to sustain the platform, not to make the free tier feel incomplete
- If we ever need to change this commitment, we'll communicate it openly and in advance

### 2. Honest About What We Are

ImpactMojo is a small ed-tech platform, not a university or accredited institution. We're transparent about this:
- Our certificates certify course completion only — they carry no institutional or regulatory weight
- Our tools are designed for practitioners, not enterprise-grade software
- Our content reflects best practices but may not apply to every local context
- We have a small team, which means fast iteration but also limitations in support capacity

### 3. Content Accuracy

We take factual accuracy seriously because development professionals make real decisions based on what they learn:
- All course content is written or reviewed by Dr. Varna Sri Raman (PhD, Development Economics)
- Statistics and citations reference published, verifiable sources
- When we use AI in content development, it goes through multi-stage expert review (see [AI Policy](ai-policy-guide.md))
- When users report errors, we investigate and correct them

### 4. No Misleading Claims

- We don't fabricate user numbers, testimonials, or impact metrics
- We don't claim institutional affiliations or accreditations we don't have
- We don't promise outcomes (jobs, skills, career advancement) that we can't guarantee
- Our pricing is straightforward — no hidden fees, no surprise charges

## How We Sustain the Platform

ImpactMojo generates revenue through:

| Revenue source | What it is |
|---------------|-----------|
| **Premium memberships** | Practitioner (₹399/mo), Professional (₹999/mo), Organization (₹1,499/user/mo) |
| **Workshops** | Cohort-based training at ₹12,000–15,000 per cohort |
| **Coaching** | One-on-one sessions at ₹1,500–2,500 per session |
| **Dojos** | Practice sessions at ₹1,500 per person per session |
| **Voluntary contributions** | "Pay What You Think Is Fair" via UPI |

This model means:
- We're not dependent on a single donor or grant
- Free content isn't funded by advertising or data monetisation
- We don't sell user data or use it for marketing
- Sliding-scale pricing ensures grassroots organisations aren't excluded

## What We Track (And What We Don't)

### We track:
- Anonymous usage analytics (which pages are visited, how long, via Google Analytics)
- Course completion rates (for logged-in users)
- Platform performance (load times, errors)

### We don't track:
- Individual browsing behaviour for marketing purposes
- Personal data beyond what you voluntarily provide (name, email)
- Learning activity for the purpose of training AI models

See the [Privacy Policy](privacy-guide.md) for full details.

## How to Hold Us Accountable

If you believe ImpactMojo is not living up to these commitments:

1. **Report it.** Email [hello@impactmojo.in](mailto:hello@impactmojo.in) or open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/Varnasr/ImpactMojo/issues).
2. **We'll respond.** Within 2 business days.
3. **We'll fix it.** If we've made an error or failed to meet a commitment, we'll correct it and be transparent about what happened.

We'd rather hear about a problem and fix it than have it erode trust silently.
