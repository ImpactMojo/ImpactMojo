What Are Book Summaries?

Book Summaries are interactive, chapter-by-chapter companions for essential development texts. Unlike traditional summaries, these are designed for practitioners who want to understand and apply the ideas, not just skim them.

  • Chapter-by-chapter navigation with key arguments and evidence
  • Interactive elements to test comprehension and connect ideas
  • Practitioner-focused takeaways for fieldwork and programme design
  • Completely free and open access — no login required

All Book Summaries are part of ImpactMojo's free Specials collection, alongside games, labs, and interactive tools.
