General Questions

ImpactMojo is a free educational platform designed specifically for development professionals, NGOs, and impact practitioners across South Asia. We offer comprehensive courses on MEAL (Monitoring, Evaluation, Accountability, and Learning), Theory of Change, research methods, gender studies, development economics, and public health.

Our mission is to democratize development education by making high-quality learning resources accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial situation or location.

Yes, absolutely! All our core educational content is free and always will be. This includes:

  • 39+ comprehensive courses
  • 10 interactive labs
  • 12 learning games
  • Learning Loops blog
  • Between the Logframes podcast
  • Research tools and calculators

We follow a "pay what you think is fair" philosophy. If our resources help you, you're welcome to contribute any amount via UPI (impactmojo@ibl), but it's entirely optional. Your access to learning is never dependent on payment.

ImpactMojo was founded by Dr. Varna Sri Raman, a development economist.

The platform is sponsored by PinPoint Ventures, whose support enables us to keep all educational content free and accessible.

ImpactMojo is designed for anyone working in or interested in the development sector:

  • NGO professionals looking to strengthen their MEAL and program design skills
  • Development practitioners seeking to deepen their theoretical understanding
  • Students pursuing careers in international development, public policy, or social work
  • Government officials involved in program monitoring and evaluation
  • CSR professionals designing and measuring corporate social impact
  • Donors and funders who want to better understand impact measurement
  • Career changers transitioning into the development sector

Courses & Learning

We currently offer 39+ courses across six learning tracks:

  • MEAL Track - Monitoring, Evaluation, Accountability, and Learning
  • Theory of Change Track - Program design and logic models
  • Research Methods Track - Qualitative, quantitative, and mixed methods
  • Gender & Inclusion Track - Gender analysis and inclusive programming
  • Development Economics Track - Economic principles for development
  • Public Health Track - Health systems and program evaluation

In addition to courses, we offer 10 interactive labs for hands-on practice and 12 learning games to make learning engaging. Visit our Course Catalog to explore all available courses.

Workshops & Live Sessions

We offer both live virtual workshops and in-person training sessions on topics including:

  • Data & Impact Reporting
  • Theory of Change Development
  • MEAL Framework Design
  • Qualitative Research Methods
  • Survey Design & Analysis
  • Gender Analysis Tools

Workshops typically run 2-4 hours and include interactive exercises, case studies, and practical takeaways. Check our Workshops page for upcoming sessions.

Unlike our self-paced courses, workshops typically require a registration fee to cover facilitator time and materials. However:

  • Scholarships are available for those who need financial assistance
  • We regularly host free community events and webinars
  • Group discounts are available for organizations
  • Premium members receive discounted workshop rates

Contact us at hello@impactmojo.in to inquire about scholarships or group rates.

Our workshop refund policy is based on how far in advance you cancel:

  • More than 7 days before: 100% refund
  • 3-7 days before: 50% refund
  • Less than 3 days before: No refund (but you can transfer your spot to a colleague)

For full details, please see our Refund Policy.

Certificates

Yes! We offer several types of certificates:

  • Course Completion Certificates: Awarded when you complete a course and pass any associated assessments
  • Workshop Participation Certificates: Provided after attending live workshops
  • Track Completion Certificates: Awarded when you complete all courses in a learning track

Certificates include your name, course/workshop title, completion date, and a unique verification code.

Premium certificate options (including physical printed copies and LinkedIn integration) may be available for premium members or for a small fee.

Technical Questions

ImpactMojo is designed to work on all modern devices:

  • Desktop computers (Windows, Mac, Linux)
  • Laptops
  • Tablets (iPad, Android tablets)
  • Mobile phones (iOS and Android)

We recommend using the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge for the best experience.

Currently, our courses are designed for online access. However:

  • You can download handouts and supplementary materials from many courses
  • You can save pages for offline reading using your browser's save feature
  • Our platform is optimized for low-bandwidth access

We're exploring offline capabilities for future updates to better serve users with limited internet access.

Try these troubleshooting steps first:

  • Refresh the page or clear your browser cache
  • Try a different browser or device
  • Check your internet connection
  • Ask Mojini, our AI assistant, using the chat button on the bottom right

If issues persist, email us at hello@impactmojo.in with:

  • A description of the problem
  • Screenshots if possible
  • Your browser and device information

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data at any time. To do so:

  • Email us at hello@impactmojo.in with subject line "Data Deletion Request"
  • Or use the form on our Data Protection page

We will process your request within 30 days in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.
