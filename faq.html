What is ImpactMojo?

ImpactMojo is a free development education platform designed specifically for NGOs, social impact organizations, and development practitioners across South Asia. We offer courses, labs, games, and resources on MEAL, Theory of Change, Research Methods, Gender Studies, and more.

Are all courses really free?

Yes! All core educational content on ImpactMojo is completely free and will remain free forever. We follow a "pay what you think is fair" philosophy. Our optional premium features and tools help sustain the platform while keeping education accessible to all.

Do I get a certificate after completing a course?

Yes, you will receive a digital certificate of completion for every course you finish. Our certificates include a unique verification code and can be shared on LinkedIn or downloaded as a PDF for your professional portfolio.

Do I need to create an account?

You can browse and access much of our content without an account. However, creating a free account allows you to track your progress, earn certificates, save your notes, and access personalized features. Registration only takes a minute!

What does Premium membership include?

Premium membership gives you access to advanced tools like the Research Ops Suite, Power & Sample Lab, priority support, downloadable resources, and exclusive workshops. It helps support our mission while giving you professional-grade tools for your impact work.

What languages are the courses available in?

Our courses are primarily in English, with select content available in Hindi, Tamil, Bengali, Telugu, and Marathi. We are continuously expanding our multilingual offerings to serve development professionals across South Asia.
