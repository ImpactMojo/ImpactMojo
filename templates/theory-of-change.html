Theory of Change Builder

How to use this Theory of Change builder:
  1. Work backwards from Impact — start with the change you want to see, then fill in what's needed to get there.
  2. Click "+ Add card" to add items to each column. Type directly into cards to edit.
  3. Use the Assumptions column to capture what must hold true for your logic to work.
  4. Export as JSON to save your work, or Print to create a PDF.
