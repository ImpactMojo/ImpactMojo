Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Introduction

ImpactMojo ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011 of India. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website www.impactmojo.in.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Name and email address (when you sign in with Google)
  • Educational preferences and learning progress
  • Bookmarked courses and notes
  • Feedback and course suggestions

2.2 Non-Personal Information

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website addresses

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining our educational services
  • Personalizing your learning experience
  • Saving your progress and preferences
  • Responding to your inquiries and feedback
  • Improving our content and services
  • Sending newsletter updates (only if subscribed)
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

As per the IT Act requirements:

  • Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform)
  • We implement reasonable security practices including encryption
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We maintain logs of data access as required by law
  • Data is stored on servers that may be located outside India

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information only:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With service providers who assist in operating our platform (Firebase, Plausible Analytics)

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication and data storage
  • Plausible Analytics: For privacy-friendly usage analytics (no cookies)
  • Substack: For newsletter subscriptions (separate privacy policy applies)
  • Formspree: For contact form submissions

7. Your Rights

Under Indian law, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • File a complaint with our Grievance Officer

8. Children's Privacy

ImpactMojo is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

10. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Maintaining your login session
  • Remembering your theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Firebase Authentication

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date."
